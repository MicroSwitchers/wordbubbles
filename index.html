About Word Bubbling

Word Bubbling is a tool designed to create high-contrast, outline-bubbled text based on the Roman-Lantzy method for CVI (Cortical Visual Impairment).

It helps children with CVI focus on words by reducing visual clutter and emphasising word shape through high-contrast contours.
